features:
pngs for all game elements
collisions are based off of the pngs
both wasd and arrow keys work for movement
the player can 'dive' (press down to go faster than gravity would have them go)
end screen for both victory and defeat
utilization of callbacks to change the level
utilization of callbacks in the event of a gameover
platforms have friction
characters change their sprites to match the direction of their last input
2 types of enemy
